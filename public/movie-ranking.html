The Best Movies According to Jim

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lion King (1994)

This was one of my favourite childhood movies. And it still does trigger some great childhood memories, which can all be summarized into two words - 'Hakuna matata'.

Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines

I really loved the Terminator and the fight scenes in the movie. It really made me dream of building machines that could fight and protect others.

Spider-Man (2002)

I watched this movie over and over again. Just being amazed at how Spider-Man would always swoop in and save the day. I also loved the songs and music played within the movie with lots of excitement.
